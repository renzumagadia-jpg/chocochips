Description

Bais City National Science High School (BCNSHS), located in Maria Longa Drive, Bais City, Negros Oriental, is a public science high school committed to nurturing academically strong, scientifically-minded youth. It follows the Department of Education’s Science High School curriculum, aligned with the K–12 framework, offering special programs in science, technology, and research, while encouraging excellence not only in academics but also in skills-based and extracurricular activities..
